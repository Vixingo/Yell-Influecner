How it Works

Step 1.

Go over a talent's profile to know who they are and see what they are offering. You can send them a message if you have questions about their services. You can also submit a partnership enquiry if you want to work with a talent for an extended period. Additionally, you can book their services directly from their profile.



Step 2.

As a business or client, you can post an advertisement for an opportunity or project you may have, and talents can submit their application directly from your advertisement. Make sure to provide as much information as you can in your advert. The detailed the brief, the more talents you will attract.



Step 3.

Communicate with the talents you have booked. Agree on prices, schedule and more. Let them get your business out there, attract customers, and boost your sales.



Step 4.

Some talents have pre-set rates for the services posted on their profile, which means you cannot negotiate. You can book any of those services directly from their profile.
