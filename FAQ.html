General

Booking, Fees and Payment

It is absolutely free to sign up on the platform and post an opportunity. You will only need to pay when you create a successful booking of talent. You are in control of how much you want to spend as well as the the talents you want to partner with, and they will let you know if they are interested or not in your opportunity. Take note that Influencer Hacki does not set prices for opportunities. It is the talents who decide if they would like to apply or not. Of course, talents do not only look into budgets when applying. They may also consider an opportunity if it is interesting, aligns with their passion, or if they believe that it is a good project overall.


Don't forget that the bigger the budget you allocate, the more top talents you will attract. So consider setting a maximum budget, and you will be surprised at the number of talents willing to work with you.

Once an agreement has been reached between a business and talent, Influencer Hacki asks a 50% deposit to confirm the booking. This can be refunded if the booking is cancelled 14 days before the scheduled project/opportunity or if the talent backs out for whatever reason. The remaining 50% of the fee will be payable 7 days before the scheduled project/opportunity.

For projects/opportunities with shorter turnaround times, full payment is needed to confirm booking. Full refund will be provided if a talent does not carry through with what was agreed to do or withdraw from the scheduled project/opportunity.
